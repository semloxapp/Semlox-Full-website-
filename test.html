Frequently asked questions

Before you place an order.

What do you need from me?

Usually your website or product information, logo, brand colors, offer, audience, preferred platform, and any photos or videos you want included.

Can you make an ad without existing video footage?

Yes. Depending on the project, the video can use AI-generated visuals, licensed stock media, motion graphics, supplied product images, typography, or a combination of these.

Can you create ads for any industry?

Most legal products, services, events, brands, and businesses can be supported. Restricted, deceptive, unsafe, or unlawful advertising requests are not accepted.

Will the ad be approved by every platform?

Platform approval cannot be guaranteed. Each advertising network applies its own policies, review process, targeting rules, and industry restrictions.

Do you run the advertising campaign?

The packages shown focus on creative production. Campaign setup, media buying, targeting, and ongoing management can be listed separately if those services are offered.
